Who am I

I am a dedicated and creative professional with over 5 years of experience in HTML development and graphic design. My expertise lies in crafting clean, responsive websites and visually compelling design assets using CorelDRAW. With a strong foundation in web standards and design principles, I bring both technical precision and artistic vision to every project.

I have worked on a variety of web and print projects, including portfolios, landing pages, logos, branding materials, and marketing designs. My goal is always to deliver high-quality, user-focused solutions that are not only functional but also aesthetically engaging.

I take pride in my attention to detail, problem-solving ability, and commitment to continuous learning. Whether working independently or in collaboration with a team, I strive to bring value, creativity, and efficiency to every task.
